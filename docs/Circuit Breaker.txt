Circuit Breaker 
- It is a resilience pattern used to cascading failure in a 
microservices project. Means, when one microservices fails or becomes slow,
causing all other dependent services to also fail one after another.
- It monitores calls between services.
- If one services is down or becomes too slow, then the breaker OPEN
the circuit and stop further calls.
- Instead of waiting for timeout, it return a fallback response immediately
